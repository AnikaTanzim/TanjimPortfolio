About Me :

25

YEARS OLD

In my childhood, I always dreamt to be a cricketer. I was so passionate about sports and I had shown my potential at cricket. I was a very big fan of Ricky Ponting. By the time, I had played for my district team several times.

After getting admitted to university I became more interested in poetry. So I gave a try to write poetry. My work has been published on some of the renowned magazines of our country.Each time I did something good and recognized by my work it made me confident about doing something great about poetry in my life. This is how my life gets all poetic and becomes both my hobby and my specialty.

Besides poetry, I have been always interested in photography. Mostly, I do mobile photography and love nature as my canvas.
